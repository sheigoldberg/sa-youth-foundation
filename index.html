Services

The South African Youth Foundation is a Bahai inspired organisation seeking to uplift the lives of South African youth. The organisation has 3 primary fields of service.

Health and Well Being

A Bahai inspired community clinic has been in operation since 2011 serving the local community around North Riding Johannesburg. The clinic provide primary health care for patients who cannot afford a private GP. A highly reduced fee of R20 per patient is charged in order to maintain the clinic. The majority of the costs of operation of the clinic are paid for by the local Bahai community

Education

There are a number of education intiatives happening under the SA Youth Foundation umbrella. The organisation's core belief is that empowerment through education is the most effective means for effecting positive social change. The educational initiatives focus on both the material and spiritual education of youth. More details can be found in the projects below.

Community Development

The reality of human beings and societies is fundamentally spiritual in nature and as such the SA Youth Foundation seeks to tap into this psiritual nature of its participants to develop and build the communities within which they live. Participants partake in prayer gatherings and educational initiatives to assist with the spiritual and moral growth of the members of the community.

Arts

One of the most powerful ways to inspire and generate energy for positive change is through the arts. Engaging youth through dramatic and musical means can prove a powerful way to facilitate conversations and open up minds. There are a number of art based initiatives under the SA Youth Foundation. More details can be found in the projects below.

Race Relations

One of the major social issues facing South African Youth is the still present split of society along racial lines. Interweaved throughout all SA Youth Foundations activities seeking to uplift youth and their communities is the theme of building love and bonds of friendship across racial boundaries and seeking to heal societal divisions entrenched over the course of decades.

Finance

Income generation and financial independance projects. We are currently working toward the ability to offer bursaries and scholarships and provide a pool of financial resources to empower youth involved in other intiatives related to the SA Youth Foundation
